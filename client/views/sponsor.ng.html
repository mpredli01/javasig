Our Sponsor

Our sponsor is the Amateur Computer Group of New Jersey (ACGNJ), a computer user group (club) based in Scotch Plains, New Jersey. ACGNJ, founded in May 1975 by Sol Libes , is the oldest computer club still in operation. The club is comprised of a number of special interest groups (SIGs) which have meetings throughout the month. ACGNJ (along with several other organizations) also sponsors and contributes to produce the annual Trenton Computer Festival (TCF).
